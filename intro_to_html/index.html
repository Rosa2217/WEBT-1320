The Hunger Games

There are 12 districts in the movie and each year one girl and one boy by the age of 12 and above are chosen to compete in a game of life and death. The movie starts off in District twelve when a girl named Katniss is hunting wild animals to sell at the supermarket for food and supplies. The time has come to choose a girl and a boy to compete in the 75th games. Katniss's sister is chosen in her first year, but Katniss volunteered to take her place. Katniss is send off with one of the boys named Pettafrom her district to train and learn about the games. There is only one winner to the games, but for some reason the rules changed and there can be 2 winners only from the same district. When this new rule is given, Katniss and Petta have work together to survive and leave together.

Characters

Katniss is the main character is the movie. She is very talented with a bow and arrow, which comes in handy when she is in the games. It's very hard for her to trust people, but later learnes to trust Petta and Haymitch. The two people who help her survive.Petta is another of the main charcaters in the movie. He is very talented with realistic art since he's a baker back in the district. This is very useful to him because he uses it to camoflauge himself in the games. He is also very strong since he had to carry heavy bags of flour and sugar back home.
