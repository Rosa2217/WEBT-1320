The Hunger Games: Characters

Katniss Everdeen

Portrayed by: Jennifer Lawarence

Katniss is a tribute from District 12. She is very independent, resourceful, fiercely protective, and determined. Katniss is a natural survivor with a deep love for her family, especially her younger sister Prim. She does not feel comfortable with being a symbol or a leader but takes on the role out of necessity. She is talented with a bow and arrow and always has her hair braided. Her style is simple with jeans, a t-shirt, a jacket, and her outdoor boots, all preferred in black.

Peeta Mellark

Portrayed by: Josh Hutcherson

Peeta Mellark is also a tribute from District 12 and is Katniss's fellow competitor. He is kind-hearted, selfless, and charismatic. Peeta is known for his ability to charm and manipulate the public, though his motivations are rooted in love fo Katniss. He's a skilled baker with a natural talent for speaking under pressure. Peeta is also optimistic, romantic, resourceful, and emotionally intelligent.Unlike Katniss he has blond hair and blue eyes.
